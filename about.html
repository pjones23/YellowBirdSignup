About Us

We are rethinking the way financial education works. We don’t teach from a classroom, we don’t show long videos or write 500-page books to read. Instead, we are a mobile-specific, interactive solution.

YellowBird was founded in Atlanta, Georgia in 2014 by a small team of entrepreneurs with a variety of investing backgrounds. The methods in which the founders were taught did not speak to their demographic in terms relevant to them, nor to their mobile emphasis. Learning should not be confined to traditional methods only.
